## 8. Risk Mitigation

### 8.1 Financial Risks

| Risk | Mitigation | Reserve |
|------|------------|---------|
| **Lower revenue** | Conservative projections, 20% buffer | $20,000 |
| **Higher CAC** | Diversified channels, organic growth focus | $15,000 |
| **Longer sales cycles** | Pilot programs, success guarantees | $10,000 |
| **Technical issues** | Comprehensive testing, monitoring | $5,000 |

### 8.2 Contingency Plans

**If Revenue is 50% of Target:**
- Reduce marketing spend by 30%
- Delay non-critical hires
- Extend runway by 3 months
- Focus on high-value customers

**If Revenue is 150% of Target:**
- Accelerate hiring plans
- Increase infrastructure capacity
- Expand to additional cities faster
- Invest in premium features
